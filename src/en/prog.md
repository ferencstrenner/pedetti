# Programs and attractions around Balatonfüred

Balatonfüred and the surrounding area offer numerous recreational opportunities for visitors. Whether you're planning a vacation or just a weekend getaway, you're sure to find interesting programs.

## Beach and swimming

- **Eszterházy Beach**: The city's largest and most popular beach with grassy sunbathing areas, slides and many services.
- **Kisfaludy Beach**: Quieter, family-friendly environment directly on the lakefront.

## Cultural programs

- **Vaszary Gallery**: Contemporary art exhibitions.
- **Jókai Memorial House**: The former home of the famous Hungarian writer, now a museum.
- **Tagore Promenade**: Romantic walk along Lake Balaton, where famous poets once strolled.

## Wine and gastronomy

- **Balaton wine routes**: Visit local wineries and taste the excellent wines of the region.
- **Local restaurants**: Fish restaurants, modern Hungarian cuisine, Balaton specialties.

## Active programs

- **Cycling**: Lake Balaton circular cycle route. You can rent bicycles at [FüredBike](https://furedbike.hu/), which offers a wide selection of simple city bikes, e-bikes and child seats.
- **Water sports**: Sailing, SUP, kayak and canoe rental.
- **Tihany**: Visit the nearby Tihany Peninsula and the famous Benedictine Abbey.

## Family programs

- **Balatonfüred Adventure Park**: Rope courses and climbing walls for all ages.
- **Boat trips**: Explore Lake Balaton by ferry or motor boat and enjoy the beauty of the lake from the water.

More information: [Balatonfüred Tourism](https://www.balatonfured.hu/)
